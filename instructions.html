Jeopardy

Game Objective:

Compete with another player to answer trivia questions correctly and earn points. The player with the most points at the end wins.

Setup:

  1. Enter the names of Player 1 and Player 2 in the input fields.
  2. Categories and question cards are displayed on the board.
  3. Click on a card to reveal a question.

Gameplay:

  • Read the question and provide your answer.
  • If the answer is correct, earn points.
  • Click the Escape key to return to the board after answering.
  • Continue playing until all questions are answered. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins.
